# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We pledge that everyone participating in the CTECX-DigitalSign community and its communities will be treated with respect.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

- **Be Respectful** - Value each other's ideas, styles and viewpoints
- **Be Direct but Professional** - We are all professionals, conduct conversations with professionalism
- **Be Inclusive** - Seek diverse perspectives and encourage, support and respect voices of all
- **Be Community Minded** - We're here to lift each other up
- **Be Patient** - Remember that communication happens across different time zones
- **Understand Disagreements** - Disagreement is natural, but doesn't excuse poor behavior or poor communication
- **Be Considerate** - Think before you write; be courteous to others
- **Be Clear** - Avoid sarcasm and ambiguous language; assume good intent
- **Give Back** - Look for ways to contribute and help others succeed

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Insulting, demeaning language or personal attacks
- Discrimination based on race, gender, sexual orientation, disability, age, religion, etc.
- Threats of violence or violent language
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Harassment or intimidation
- Advocating for or encouraging any of the above behaviors
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate

## Our Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying the standards of acceptable behavior and are expected to take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any instances of unacceptable behavior.

Project maintainers have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned to this Code of Conduct, or to ban temporarily or permanently any contributor for other behaviors that they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies:

- Within project spaces (issues, pull requests, discussions)
- In community spaces when an individual is representing the project or community
- Outside of project spaces if the behavior has a direct negative impact on the project community

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project team at ctaxnagomi@gmail.com. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated.

The project team will:
- Respond to reports within 72 hours
- Keep reporter identity confidential if requested
- Take appropriate action based on findings
