✍️Standard Digital Signature

Create a professional digital signature directly on our canvas. Perfect for signing documents, contracts, and approvals. Your signature stays local on your device - never stored on our servers.

  1. Open the Signature Canvas

    Go to the home page and scroll down to the "Sign Your Document" section. You'll see a blank canvas ready for your signature.

  2. Customize Your Pen Settings

    Use the controls on the left:

    • Pen Size: Adjust from 1 to 20 pixels using the slider
    • Pen Color: Click the color picker to choose your preferred color
    • Background: Select transparent, white, or light background

  3. Sign on the Canvas

    Click and drag on the white canvas area to draw your signature. Your handwriting will be rendered smoothly with natural curves. You can sign as many times as you like - just use the Clear button to start over.

  4. Download Your Signature

    Once satisfied with your signature, choose your preferred format:

    • PNG - Best quality, supports transparency
    • JPG - Smaller file size, no transparency
    • WebP - Modern format, best compression, transparent

  5. Use Your Signature

    Insert the downloaded signature into documents, emails, contracts, or digital forms. Your signature is now ready for use anywhere.

🤖AI-Integrated Digital Signature

Upload a PDF document and let our AI automatically detect signature fields, guide you to the right location, and help you sign your documents efficiently. Perfect for batch document signing and forms.

  1. Navigate to AI-Integrated Signing

    Click "AI INTEGRATED" in the main navigation menu to access the advanced signing tool.

  2. Upload Your PDF Document

    Click the upload button and select your PDF file. Supported file types:

    • PDF files (.pdf)
    • Maximum file size: 50MB
    • Multi-page documents supported

  3. AI Detects Signature Fields

    Our AI analyzes your document and automatically identifies:

    • Signature boxes and lines
    • Form fields requiring signatures
    • Common signature locations
    • Confidence scores for each detection

  4. Review & Adjust Detections

    The AI shows all detected signature fields with green boxes. You can:

    • Accept AI suggestions (click ✓)
    • Reject incorrect detections (click ✗)
    • Manually add missing signature fields
    • Adjust field boundaries if needed

  5. Sign Each Field

    Click on each detected or marked signature field. A signature canvas will appear where you can:

    • Draw your signature
    • Customize pen settings
    • Undo and redo as needed
    • Clear and retry

  6. Review & Confirm

    Review all signatures on the document. Each signature field shows:

    • ✅ Status indicator
    • Signature preview
    • Timestamp (optional)
    • Edit option to re-sign

  7. Download Signed Document

    Once all signatures are complete, download your fully signed PDF with all signatures embedded.

💡Pro Tips & Tricks

💥 Tip 1: Practice Your Signature

Sign in the same way each time for consistency. Use the Clear button to practice before downloading.

🎨 Tip 2: Find Your Style

Experiment with different pen sizes (try 4-8 for most signatures) and colors. Blue and black are more professional.

🖌️ Tip 3: Smooth Curves

Move your pen/finger smoothly and deliberately. Avoid jerky movements. Our AI smooths curves automatically.

📱 Tip 4: Mobile Signing

Use a stylus or finger. Start from top-left corner and sign from left to right for best results on touchscreens.

📋 Tip 5: PDF Tips

For AI-integrated signing, ensure your PDF has clear signature fields. The AI works best with standard document layouts.

🔒 Tip 6: Privacy First

Your documents and signatures never leave your device. Clear your browser history periodically for extra security.

⚡ Tip 7: Performance

For large PDFs (10+ MB), the AI might take a few seconds. This is normal and ensures accurate detection.

💾 Tip 8: Format Selection

Use PNG for transparency, JPG for smaller file sizes, or WebP for modern web use.

❓Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Where is my data stored?

A: All your signatures and documents are stored exclusively on your device in your browser's local storage. We never store anything on our servers. Your privacy is guaranteed.

Q: Can I use the same signature multiple times?

A: Yes! Download your signature once and use it multiple times across different documents and applications.

Q: Is my signature legally binding?

A: CTECX-DigitalSign creates valid digital signatures. However, legal validity depends on your jurisdiction and how it's used. Consult local laws for specific use cases.

Q: How do I delete my data?

A: Simply clear your browser's local storage and cookies. Or use the "Clear All Data" button in Settings. Your data is immediately deleted.

Q: Can I edit my signature after signing?

A: Yes! In both standard and AI-integrated modes, you can click "Clear" and re-sign. You can also adjust detected fields in AI mode before signing.

Q: What PDF file sizes are supported?

A: We support PDF files up to 50MB. Larger files will take longer to process with AI detection.

Q: Does CTECX track my activity?

A: No. We use only essential cookies. We don't track, profile, or analyze your behavior. See our PDPR Policy for details.

Q: Can I use this offline?

A: Yes! The standard signature tool works completely offline. AI-integrated signing requires internet for the first load but works offline after that.

Q: Which browsers are supported?

A: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge, and any modern browser supporting HTML5 Canvas. Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile.

Q: How can I provide feedback or report issues?

A: Visit our GitHub repository or email us at support@ctecx.com. We'd love to hear from you!
